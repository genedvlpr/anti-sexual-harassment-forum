About Us

We aspire to a world in which individuals are empowered and mobilized to share responsibility
in creating communities free from sexualized violence and abuse. We provide a leadership role
in raising awareness, supporting those who have experienced sexualized violence, holding sexual
perpetrators accountable, and influencing social and systemic change.
